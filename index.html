The PROFET project is a three year project that receives funding from the Internet Privatstiftung Austria (IPA) under the netidee SCIENCE program under Austrian Science Fund (FWF) project number P 31621-N38.

The project is led by Dr. Daniel Slamanig at the AIT Austrian Institute of Technology and the research is carried out together with the Security and Privacy group from Prof. Matteo Maffei at TU Wien.
